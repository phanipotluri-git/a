The Formula

10 compounds.
One synergistic system.

Each active in Aeternyx™ is selected not for trend, but for evidence. Every compound is dosed at clinically relevant concentrations, peer-reviewed, and positioned to work synergistically with the rest of the formula.

01
Astaxanthin
Master Antioxidant
6,000× more potent than Vitamin C in singlet oxygen quenching. Crosses blood-brain and blood-retinal barriers. Protects against UV-induced oxidative stress and neuroinflammation.
02
Lycopene
Carotenoid · Cancer Shield
Finnish Cohort data demonstrates 55% reduction in prostate cancer risk. Lipid-soluble antioxidant with superior vascular and DNA-protective properties.
03
Trans-Resveratrol
Sirtuin Activator · Longevity
Georgetown Phase II validated cardiovascular safety profile. Activates SIRT1 longevity pathway, modulates NF-κB, and mimics caloric restriction effects at the cellular level.
04
Tocotrienol
Superior Vitamin E · Neuro
40-60× more potent than tocopherols in antioxidant activity. Neuroprotective, anti-angiogenic, and a validated anti-inflammatory via NF-κB suppression.
05
Alpha Lipoic Acid
Universal Antioxidant
Both water and fat soluble — regenerates Vitamins C and E within the antioxidant network. Critical for mitochondrial bioenergetics and heavy metal chelation.
06
CoQ10
Mitochondrial Energetics
Q-SYMBIO RCT: 43% reduction in major cardiovascular events in heart failure patients. Essential for mitochondrial ATP synthesis; depleted by statins and ageing.
07
Vitamin C
Immune Backbone · Collagen
Ascorbic acid at therapeutic dose. Regenerates Vitamins E and glutathione; essential for collagen synthesis, immune surveillance, and iron absorption.
08
Vitamin D3
Immune Modulator · Bone
BMJ meta-analysis (11,321 participants): 42% reduction in acute respiratory infections. Regulates over 200 genes. Deficiency epidemic across urban India.
09
Vitamin K2 MK-7
Cardiovascular · Bone Matrix
Rotterdam Study: High K2 intake associated with 57% reduction in cardiovascular mortality. Activates osteocalcin and Matrix Gla Protein to direct calcium away from arteries.
10
Selenium
Thyroid · Oncology Shield
NPC Trial (JAMA, 1312 patients): 63% reduction in prostate, 58% colorectal, 46% lung cancer incidence. Core cofactor for glutathione peroxidase and thyroid hormone activation.
